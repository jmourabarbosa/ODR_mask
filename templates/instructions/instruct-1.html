Overall Instructions

You will be asked to perform a task which will probe your working memory precision. It's been reported in the scientific literature that working memory capacity its highly correlated with IQ.

Upon experiment termination, you will be told by email how was your performance relative to all the other participants. Do not try to cheat if you want to get a real report on how good you are. The final bonus does not strictly depend on your performance, but if we find out you cheated or answered randombly you will not recieve any bonus.

On top of the base rate, you will recieve 0.5cent per memory trial. There is an extra bonus of 10% if you do more than 500 trials. If you reach the 500 trials roof without answering randomly, you will get a total bonus of $2.75. 500 trials takes less than 15min to complete. If you reach a roof of 1000 trials, you will get a total bonus of $5.5.

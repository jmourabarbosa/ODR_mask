Instructions for training

Final test

You will now see a small set of trials. Feel free to try out until you understand how the task works. When you complete the training at least once, you can start the real experiment.

On top of the $0.1 base rate, you will recieve 0.5cent per memory trial. There is an extra bonus of 10% if you do more than 500 trials. If you reach the 500 trials roof without answering randomly, you will get a total bonus of $2.75. 500 trials takes less than 15min to complete. If you reach a roof of 1000 trials, you will get a total bonus of $5.5.

Good luck!

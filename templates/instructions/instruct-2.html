Begining of each trial

At the beginning of each trial you need to reposition your mouse pointer on the central empty box. When the pointer is over the box, the trial will be initiated. Remember to focus on the box for the whole trial duration in order deliver real performance data.

If you remove your mouse from the center when the box is black, you will be penalized by 5cent on your final bonus. If you do it more often, you will be banned from the experiment.
